About RO IT Systems

A consulting practice built on trust, discipline, and execution.

The practice

RO IT Systems began as a secure integration and enterprise architecture consultancy focused on hardened network-enabled systems. Its current work extends that foundation into responsible AI, data governance, platform modernisation, and agentic AI adoption.

RO IT Systems is led by Morgane Oger, a senior technology leader and consultant working at the intersection of responsible AI, data governance, enterprise platforms, integration architecture, and public trust.

Morgane brings more than 20 years of experience across enterprise data platforms, integration systems, DevOps, regulated environments, cloud modernisation, financial services, retail technology, and public-interest governance. Her work combines executive-level strategy with hands-on architecture and delivery experience.

Her background includes leadership of enterprise data and integration platforms, generative AI governance and safe-use work, Snowflake and Azure modernisation, MuleSoft/API ecosystems, large-scale ETL/ELT environments, and consulting across banking, telecom, utilities, retail, and advanced technology.

Morgane also brings public-interest governance experience as the founder of the Morgane Oger Foundation, a board leader, human-rights advocate, and recipient of the Meritorious Service Medal. That perspective shapes a consulting approach focused on trust, accountability, and practical implementation.
